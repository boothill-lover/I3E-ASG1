# I3E ASG1
You are hungry. You are in a hallucination where everything looks like food because you are so hungry. Objective is to eat 8 actual food items to restore reality, then go through a final door that only unlocks when all food items are consumed.

# Controls
WASD to move
E to interact
Space to jump
Shift to sprint

# Hardware and Platform Required
Windows PC, minimal requirements

# Limitations and Bugs
- Doors will slam in your face
- Puzzles can be skippable
- You cannot break a jar and collect the food right after, you need to get out of range then enter back in
- If you took DOT and you die, when respawning sometimes the DOT isn't over and you end up taking damage.

# Brief Walkthrough
## Area 1
- x1 Food in the pot
- x1 Food in the freezer
- x1 Food around the corner on the far left
- x1 Food behind the pots
- x2 Foods in a Jar

Open the door to enter the crossroads.

On the left there is a small gap which leads you to a secret area.
Jump on the various platforms and you can find
- x2 Foods

Upon exiting the area, straight ahead is Area 2, the big pot of soup.
## Area 2
- x1 Food
- x1 Hammer (Used to break foods in jars)

Straight ahead at the crossroads will be Area 3.
## Area 3
- x1 Food at the back of the room.
- x2 Pressure plates
- x1 Big Apple (Pushable)

Push the big apple onto the pressure plate and stand on the other one to open the door.

Enter the final door to win the game and view your final score.
